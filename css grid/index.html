WELCOME LADIES AND GENTLEMEN! Here is some stuff about where I grew up and when we moved. So, I grew up in the town of Sandy UT, a very quaint and cold place. My parents loved to take me and my younger brother to the local frozen yogurt place so we could go get a treat. It was a fun way to pass the time and go get something tasty. We enjoyed living there, in our cozy townhome. As years passed, my brother and I kept getting very sick every winter due to the fact that SLC became a literal place where all you could smell was car exhaust. Safe to understand why we would always get sick. So, in 2015, we said goodbye to our townhome, and we moved here to Ivins. We resided in a lovely home where we have lived for about 10 years now. Crazy how time flies. And we have loved every second of it.


So, as you will see below, I sort of explained some favorite things. But, I will go into more detail of everything else I enjoy. I love cars so much. Always have since I was younger, courtesy of my dad. Some of my favorite cars however, are my dad's 2004 Cadillac CTSV stick shift. I really love how it sounds, the V8 on it from a Corvette really makes it unique. It also goes fast, and I really enjoy that too. I also love a 1968 Dodge Charger with the outtake on the hood. Those muscle cars are very cool and nice to look at, online of course. I have also ridden inside of a Bugatti Veyron. And if you want to know, yes, it was amazing. It goes 0-60 in less than 2 seconds and its W-16 engine puts power and horsepower into that car like you have never seen. Some other things I enjoy are guitar. I love playing my electric guitar to lots of good songs.. and learning new ones along the way! Some current favorites have to be.. Californication by RHCP, Otherside by RHCP, Gimme, Gimme, Gimme by ABBA, Seven Nation Army, and a bunch of others. I also have the passion for vinyls. And I have for a few years now. And for christmas, I was gifted a beautiful T1 turntable that I have in my room. I have all sorts of vinyls that I use to play on the turntable, and the music plays through the house which I like. I also collect Jordans too, and I have quite a few pairs of those, also courtesy to my dad. Overall, I also love travelling with my family too. We have been all over, from in the states, to out of country. Out of country, I have been to Paris France, London about 2 times, Venice Italy, 4 places in Switzerland, and we continue to travel every chance we get.
