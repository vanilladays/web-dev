Our History

Our cat cafe started out as an idea, a dream. And we wanted to turn it into a reality. So, we got our team, got some cats, and went through a lot of hard work to get the Cloudy Cat Cafe up and running. We hope it brings smiles to people's faces, and that we can continue to expand, and go beyond. So, stop by and enjoy our cafe, pet some cats, and get some great quality food.
