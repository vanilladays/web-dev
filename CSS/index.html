The Magic of Borough Market, London.

Home Attractions

Welcome to my favorite place! The Borough Market in London, England.

The Borough Market is a super cool and unique outdoor market that you can wander and explore, and Borough Market has a lot of good stalls there to purchase from; as well as some good restaurants and treats.

What to do:

At the Borough Market, there is a lot you can do there! You can shop, look around, grab a bite to eat, explore the different stalls, get some produce, some gifts, get a food tour. It's a fun, food involved market that has some attractions/activites nearby. Such as the London Bridge Experience, the Southwark Cathedral, the Shard etc. And around Midday, they have live cooking demos that you can watch and the chefs show you how to properly make the food and you can taste what they make!

Fun facts:

  • It's in London's Oldest Suburb
  • It became a Pure Food Market
  • The Borough Market is quite the filming location! 3 movies have been filmed there
  • The Market is known for Bread Ahead's fluffy doughnuts filled with custard or jam
  • Borough Market has been open for over 250 years!

The Seasons at the Borough Market!

During the seasons at the Borough Market, there are all sorts of things you can buy or do. In the Summertime: like June, July, August, the Market will have delicacies like courgettes, peas, cherries and berries, soft cheeses, ice cream, seasonal vegetables, etc. In May, they have more seasonal vegetables, wild garlic, milk, dairy products. In the wintertime on the other hand, they have apples, citrus fruits, apples, hard cheeses and thats about it. But, all the seasons have different delicacies, so it all depends on what season you visit.

My Personal Recommendations

  • Eat at Bread Ahead
  • Go to the Shard
  • Enjoy some fresh pasta at Padella
  • Go frolick around and explore the stalls
  • Get a tour if available
  • If possible, see if you can watch/taste test one of their live cooking demos!
